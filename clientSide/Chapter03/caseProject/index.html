FNAF stands for "Five Nights at Freddy's," which is a popular indie horror video game franchise created by Scott Cawthon. In the game, players typically take on the role of a night security guard at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza, a fictional family restaurant similar to Chuck E. Cheese's. The gameplay involves surviving five nights (and often additional nights) by monitoring security cameras and closing security doors to defend against animatronic characters that come to life at night and may attack the player. The series gained widespread popularity due to its unique blend of horror, suspense, and gameplay mechanics, as well as its intriguing lore and backstory, which are gradually revealed through clues and hidden messages throughout the games. FNAF has spawned numerous sequels, spin-offs, merchandise, and a devoted fanbase.
